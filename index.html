Overview

PIRAMAL ARANYA

At Rani Baug, Byculla

Adjacent to the serene environs of Rani Baug, Piramal Aranya is home to those who relish a lifestyle that demonstrates exclusivity and sophistication from every nook and cranny. The deep blue waters of the Arabian Sea shimmering to the East, the development offers spectacular views as far as the eye can see. These luxury apartments in Byculla come with contemporary designs that embrace cutting-edge trends with the latest technology. The presence of floor-to-ceiling windows allows the natural light to fill in the rooms. With mesmerizing views engulfing every corner, residences in Piramal Aranya are crafted with vibrant materials to create a bespoke living experience. Providing convenience and peace of mind all at once, the dwellings deliver an ideal nurturing environment.
